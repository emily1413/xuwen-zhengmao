公司简介

徐闻县正茂蔬菜种植有限公司成立于2007年，斩获《广东省农业重点龙头企业》等十几项荣誉称号，是一家专注各类果菜种植、销售及进出口业务的综合性农业企业，同时为供港澳出口原料备案种植基地。

公司形成科研、生产、加工、储运、购销一体化的规范化、产业化经营模式，拥有国家工商行政管理局、商标局签发的“新科正茂园”和“正茂园”两大注册商标；旗下农产品被湛江市检验检疫局认定为“无公害食品”，6个产品获国家农业局绿色食品认证中心“绿色食品”认证，同时拥有有机草莓专利证书等10项高新科技专利。

Company Profile

Founded in 2007, Xuwen Zhengmao Vegetable Planting Co., Ltd. has won more than ten honorary titles including "Key Leading Agricultural Enterprise of Guangdong Province". It is a comprehensive agricultural enterprise focusing on the planting, sales, import and export of various fruits and vegetables, and also a recorded planting base for export raw materials to Hong Kong and Macau.

The company has formed a standardized and industrialized operation model integrating R&D, production, processing, storage and transportation, distribution and sales. It owns two registered trademarks "Xinke Zhengmaoyuan" and "Zhengmaoyuan". Its agricultural products are recognized as "Pollution-free Food" and 6 products have obtained "Green Food" certification.

企业宗旨

遵循绿色以及低碳环保，循环可持续发展的理念，提供绿色食品，提高生活品质，传播健康理念。

Corporate Purpose

Abide by the concept of green, low-carbon environmental protection and circular sustainable development, provide green food, improve the quality of life, and spread the concept of health.

明星产品

无核荔枝

热带与亚热带珍稀果树新品种，常绿乔木，产量极其稀少，果实歪心形，平均单果重30克，果皮鲜红，无核性状稳定，品质优异。

Star Product

Seedless Litchi

A rare new variety of fruit tree in tropical and subtropical regions, an evergreen arbor with extremely scarce yield. The fruit is obcordate, with an average single fruit weight of 30 grams, bright red peel, stable seedless character and excellent quality.
